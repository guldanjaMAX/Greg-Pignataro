Origin

The arc is always the same.

A false assumption. Something shatters. And something truer gets uncovered underneath. This has happened repeatedly in Greg's life: with soccer, with purpose, with childhood trauma, with marriage, with his own body image, and with himself.

He is not a healer, coach, therapist, or trainer in the conventional sense. He is someone who teaches people that their body was never the enemy and proves it experientially, in a single afternoon.

Every key moment in Greg's life follows the same arc: a false assumption is discovered, something truer emerges, and what felt like destruction turns out to be the most important doorway he ever walked through.

Age 18
Torn ACL at Marquette University during a routine pass. What looked like the end of everything was the first real doorway. Traded the athlete identity for a deeper question: who am I without the performance?
Age 29
Three words at a bachelor party collapse the entire ego structure. A 10,000 pound weight lifts. Embraces universal peace, non separation, and unconditional love for the first time. Begins the month long inquiry that will reshape everything.
Age 33
Decades of suppressed emotion release in a 15 minute cry. Hip mobility transforms overnight. Sets the Guinness World Record for Nordic Hamstring Curls days later. 24 reps in 60 seconds. The body does not lie.
Age 35
Transforms body composition by releasing the need to look a certain way. Stands in a fitness room, handles what arrives without flinching. Radical Body Love becomes not just a philosophy but a living practice.
Now
Cofounder of Enlightened Health. World record holder. The creator of a new category of embodied transformation that dissolves the boundary between physical training and spiritual awakening. Based in Phoenix, Arizona.

The Method

This is not fitness.
This is not therapy.
This is both.

Greg combines clinical precision with philosophical depth to create embodied transformation that has no existing category. He does not teach people to use their body. He teaches people that their body was never broken.

01
Somatic Intelligence
DC electrical frequencies, NeuFit technology, and nervous system regulation to access what talk therapy cannot reach. Your body stores the story your mind has been avoiding. Greg reads the body like a text and helps you rewrite the chapters that no longer serve you.
02
Structural Liberation
ATG training principles, therapeutic movement, and biomechanical mastery combined with a philosophy that treats pain as signal, not sentence. Every restriction in the body has a message. Every limitation is an invitation to go deeper, not harder.
03
Identity Dissolution
The real transformation is not physical. It is the collapse of the false self that needed the body to perform, prove, or protect. What is left is someone who finally inhabits their own skin. Not perfected. Not optimized. Liberated.
