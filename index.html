The Method

Not fitness. Not therapy.
Something deeper.

This work lives in the space between fitness and therapy, where nobody else is looking. It does not teach you to use your body better. It helps you remember that your body was never broken in the first place.

01
Body Listening
Your body stores every story your mind has been avoiding. Through NeuFit technology and nervous system work, you will finally hear what your body has been trying to say for years. This is not talk therapy. This is your body finally getting to speak.
02
Movement as Medicine
Every tight spot, every sore place, every part of you that will not move freely is holding a message. Here, pain is treated as a signal, not a sentence. The goal is not to push harder. The goal is to listen deeper. Your body already knows the way.
03
Remembering Who You Are
The real shift is not physical. It is the moment you stop performing a version of yourself and start living as who you actually are. Not fixed. Not optimized. Free.

The Shadow

The parts you hide.

This is where the real work lives. Underneath the goals, the achievements, the curated version of yourself, there are parts that got pushed away a long time ago. Anger. Grief. Shame. Fear. The body held them when the mind could not.

Shadow work is not about fixing what is wrong. It is about welcoming what has been exiled. When you stop running from these parts, something remarkable happens: the tension that lived in your body for decades can finally let go.

This is not comfortable work. But it is the most important work you will ever do. You do not do it alone. You do it with someone who has walked this path himself. Greg is a Certified Compassionate Inquiry Practitioner trained under Dr. Gabor Maté, the world's leading voice on the connection between trauma and the body. He has sat in 7 days of complete darkness and 5 days of total silence, not because someone told him to, but because he needed to know what was underneath everything. That is the depth he brings to this work.
