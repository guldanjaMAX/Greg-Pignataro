Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Greg Pignataro ("we," "us," or "our") operates gregpignataro.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit the Site or engage with our coaching services.

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

Information you provide directly. When you book a call through Calendly, submit a contact form, or communicate with us via email, you may provide your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you choose to share.

Information collected automatically. When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain technical data through Google Analytics (GA4), including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or source
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (city/region level)
  • Interactions with site elements (clicks, scrolls)

Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect this data. We do not collect or store personally identifiable information through analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule coaching conversations
  • Understand how visitors interact with the Site so we can improve it
  • Send you information you have requested about our coaching programs
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Cookies

The Site uses cookies, which are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics GA4) to understand Site usage patterns
  • Essential cookies to remember your cookie preferences

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on the Site but will not prevent you from accessing our content.

Third Party Services

The Site integrates with the following third party services:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) for site analytics. Google's Privacy Policy
  • Calendly for scheduling. Calendly's Privacy Policy
  • YouTube for embedded video content. Google's Privacy Policy

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Analytics data is retained for 14 months in Google Analytics. If you have communicated with us about coaching services, we retain that correspondence for the duration of our professional relationship and a reasonable period afterward.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by using Google's opt out browser add on

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

The Site and our coaching services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Your continued use of the Site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
