The work behind the conversation

Most people at Necker have optimized everything in their external world. The business runs. The portfolio grows. The body keeps score differently. Somewhere along the way, the relationship with your own body became transactional: fuel it, push it, fix it, punish it.

Radical Body Love is the practice of ending that war. It draws on somatic psychology, nervous system science, and the wisdom traditions I've trained in for over 15 years. It's not about affirmations or positive thinking. It's about meeting what's actually happening in your body with the same intelligence you bring to everything else.

I've held a Guinness World Record, competed as a D1 athlete at Marquette, and trained directly under Dr. Gabor Maté. That background gives me a unique lens: I understand what it means to push the body to its absolute limits, and I understand what it costs.
